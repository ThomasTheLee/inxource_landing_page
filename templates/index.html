Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Inxource

What is Inxource?

+

Inxource is a business intelligence platform that helps you turn your data into actionable insights. We automate your order processing, organize your customer data, and provide AI-powered analytics to help you make better business decisions.

How does the automation work?

+

Our platform automatically syncs with your sales channels and processes orders in real-time. All customer purchases are instantly reflected in your dashboard without any manual data entry required.

What is Lenny AI Assistant?

+

Lenny is our AI-powered business advisor that analyzes your sales patterns, identifies seasonal trends, and provides strategic recommendations to help you grow your business more effectively.

Can I switch plans later?

+

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes will be reflected in your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences.

Is my data secure?

+

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption to protect your data and follow industry best practices for security. Your business information is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

Do you offer customer support?

+

Yes! All plans include email support. Growth and Pro plans also get priority support with faster response times. Our team is here to help you succeed.
